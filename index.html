Employer benefits

Enter Kilter Cause Challenges to compete against teams of employees from other companies around the globe! Win and prove you’re the healthiest organization out there, and do a ton of good for the communities your business serves. Here’s how it works:

  • Register team(s) – half of your entry fee goes into a donation prize pool
  • Designate your team captain(s) and round 15-50 employees per team
  • Choose 1 charity per team to compete for
  • Use Kilter Rewards to track wellness activities for the duration of the challenge. Team members earn points for completing daily goals. See scoring system.
  • Top the leaderboard and win a donation prize for your charity (HINT: Great PR Opportunity!!
