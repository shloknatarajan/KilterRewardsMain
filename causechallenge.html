Overview

What Teams Do

Teams compete for points by achieving daily fitness goals via the Kilter Rewards app. The team with the most points gets the majority of the prize pool donated to a cause of their choice!


See the Scoring System

Who You'll Help

Your Charity: Choose any cause your team wants to compete for

Yourselves: build healthier habits

Your Company: build stronger peer-to-peer relationships


Earn points for your team by completing daily fitness goals. If you're team finishes top 3, we donate a portion of the prize pool to your team's cause!

Other Perks

Team MVPs: Everyone votes for an MVP for their team. Each MVP will get a “Donate Your Hustle” t-shirt and be entered into a raffle for a $100 Amazon gift card.
Individual Donations: Take advantage of the option to make your own contributions to your team’s cause to do more good and ensure your cause benefits, no matter what. If your company offers matching donations, this is a great opportunity to double your impact.
