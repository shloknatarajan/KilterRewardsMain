What the Kilter Rewards Month of Giving Challenge Is

Teams of employees from various companies will compete in a 30 day Charity Fitness Challenge to promote and support Giving Tuesday (December 3, 2019), which is the international day of charitable giving! During this challenge, employees at participating companies will use the Kilter Rewards mobile application to track their fitness activities, earn points towards their teams’ overall score for reaching daily health goals, and work together to compete for one of five top donation prizes that will be delivered to their cause of choice on Giving Tuesday!

Start Date: November 3, 2019
End Date: December 2, 2019
Team Size Limit: 20 Participants
Team Entry Fee: $500 ($250 goes to donation prize pool!)

Want to be a challenge team?

Don't wait. Helping others by helping yourself has never been a better reason to do something. Register now!

Register

Step 1: Register your team of up to 20 employees and pay the entry fee ($250 of your team entry goes to the donation prize pool)

Step 2: Choose a cause to compete for

Step 3: Invite your colleagues to download the Kilter Rewards app and join your team using your team signup code

Step 4: During the challenge, your team will use the Kilter Rewards app to track daily fitness activities and earn up to 100 points per day for reaching their goals! At the end of the challenge, the teams with the highest average scores will earn donation prizes for their chosen causes!

Who You're Helping

Your Cause: Fundraising is really hard for non-profits today. By participating in Kilter Cause Challenges you get another way to help fund the causes you care most about! The more you help yourself and build healthier habits, the more likely you’ll be to win a donation for your cause.

Your Company: Working together with your colleagues for a common goal will strengthen relationships at work and help improve your productivity. That’s a win for you, your colleagues, and your company!
