Summer Cause Challenge

Information

Important Dates

June 15 - July 15
Teams: 7/50 Team Size: 15 - 50 members

Enter a team of 15-20 employees from your company to earn a piece of a $10,000 donation prize pool for your favorite cause!

What Teams Do

  • Choose any cause to compete for
  • Team captains send invites to team members
  • Team members download the app and enter team invite codes
  • When the challenge starts, team members use the Kilter Rewards app to track fitness activities
  • Reach daily activity goals contribute to your team’s average score
  • Then do it again tomorrow and track everything on the leaderboard!

How to Win

  • Top the wellness leaderboard! You earn points for your team by completing daily fitness goals. It’s virtual, so anyone can compete. From anywhere. However they want.
  • If you're team finishes top 3, we donate one of the following to your team's cause!
    • 1st Place: 50% of the Donation Prize Pool
    • 2nd Place: 25% of the Donation Prize Pool
    • 3rd Place: 10% of the Donation Prize Pool
    • Note: This doesn't add up to 100 and the entry fee section says 50% goes to charity garanteed so something doesn't add up

Who You'll Help

  • Your Charity: Choose any cause your team wants to compete for (recommendation: get feedback from your team on a cause you can all get behind!)
  • Yourselves: because you’ll start to build healthier habits that you’ll want to continue after this challenge.
  • Your Company: because you’ll build strong peer-to-peer relationships that last much longer than just the duration of this challenge (and you’ll probably want to do it again!)

Other Perks:

  • Team MVPs: Everyone votes for an MVP for their team. Each MVP will get a “Donate Your Hustle” t-shirt and be entered into a raffle for a $100 Amazon gift card.
  • Individual Donations: Take advantage of the option to make your own contributions to your team’s cause to do more good and ensure your cause benefits, no matter what. If your company offers matching donations, this is a great opportunity to double your impact.
