How it works

Join a Team

Looking for a new way to get in shape and help your community? Enter a team into our Charity Challenge to earn a piece of a $10,000 donation prize pool for your favorite cause!

Teams can be anywhere from 15 to 50 people and it costs $400 per team to enter.

Register

Get Active to Earn Points

Track your daily fitness activities on our Kilter Rewards app to earn points for your team. Earn more points by consistently staying active.

Contact Us

Win

At the end of the challenge, the teams with the most points get the prize pool donated to a cause of their choice.
